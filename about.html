About Us

Why Choose Us?

As a social enterprise, Evanston Development Cooperative’s inception stemmed from a desire to solve real-world issues through a sustainable business model. We believe progress is made when community members across Evanston engage in meaningful, intergenerational collaboration. Housing is at the center of any healthy community, and EDC strives to build equity in Evanston by changing the way our city thinks about it. EDC’s approach to change centers around our six core values:

Sensible housing

Diversifying the housing stock to meet the needs of all residents.

Community wealth

Approaching economic development through democratic ownership of a for-profit business.

Racial equity

Creating opportunities for net-zero homeownership and shareholding for a diverse Evanston.

Sustainability

Aligning with Evanston’s sustainability goals to achieve environmental justice through low-cost, low-footprint buildings.

Democratic ownership

Empowering community members and employees through a one-person, one-vote organization.

Job creation

Providing young Evanston residents with workforce training and a job that never leaves their hometown.

Members Evanston-based construction workers
EDC employees are trained in building high-performing homes, and each own a share of the company, ensuring transparency and input on business decisions.
Homeowners Residents from all wards of Evanston
EDC houses are designed to meet the needs of a diverse range of occupants, making us a great option for nearly anyone looking for fairly priced, reasonably sized housing.
Supporters Community members looking to make a difference
Investors with EDC keep us accountable to the goals of Evanston through being a part of our shared ownership structure.

Our Mission

EDC is a local cooperative that engages the Evanston community in intergenerational social progress by building high-performing, sensible housing. We hope to create a model for workers coops and sustainable housing that can be replicated across Cook County and Illinois. By attracting local talent to join the skilled workforce in developing properties with a community focus, EDC diversifies the housing stock and decreases Evanston residents’ carbon footprints.

Our Homes

Our homes are designed and built for a 21st century Evanston: We use durable, sustainable materials to customize agile structures for a diverse community of homeowners. Our innovative construction practices minimize utility and maintenance costs for the benefit of both the environment and our residents. EDC homes fill a growing gap in the Evanston housing stock by providing sensible, flexible solutions that change as your needs do.

What is a Coop?

In addition to catalyzing a shift in the way our city thinks about housing, EDC looks at business differently. Cooperatives allow members and owners to benefit equally from business profits in pursuit of our common goals. Unlike traditional for-profit businesses, we seek to distribute wealth more equitably among employees and community members. Democratic leadership in businesses is a powerful mechanism to ensure Evanston’s economy – and housing market – works for the many, not just a few.
