How to Use the Compatability Map
Search your Address on the map.

Click inside the search box within the map view and search for your full Evanston address.

Click on your primary house.

Click on the primary household structure on your property. (If there are several structures on your property such as a detatched garage, click on the main house.)

Consult the Compatibility Info on the Right Sidebar.

If your property shows Compatible! within the calculation section, EDC has determined that your property is a good fit for a coach house. Please contact EDC if your property does not have information available.
